YouTube URL

If you are creating a song starting from a YouTube file, you can enter the YouTube address below. The YouTube ID is also sufficient (e. g. https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=u_sIfs7Yom4

Yass Reloaded will download the audio and video files in the quality and format, you have specified in your preferences

Subtitles will also be downloaded and used as lyrics, unless they were auto-generated
